About Our Club

Who We Are

The University of Newcastle's Dungeons & Dragons Club is a community of tabletop RPG enthusiasts of all experience levels. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or have never rolled a d20 in your life, our community welcomes you!

For New Players

Never played before? No problem! Here's how to get started:

  1. Join our Discord server and introduce yourself
  2. Attend one (or both) of our weekly club nights
  3. Borrow our club materials - no need to buy anything!
  4. Join one of the many beginner-friendly one-shots that we run
